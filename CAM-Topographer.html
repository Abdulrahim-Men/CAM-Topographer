📖 How to Use CAM Topographer
  1. 1
    Export DXF from AutoCAD
    In AutoCAD, go to File → Save As and choose AutoCAD DXF (*.dxf). DXF is the open version of your drawing that this tool can read.
    File → Save As → AutoCAD DXF (*.dxf)
  2. 2
    Upload the DXF File
    Tap the upload area above and select your DXF file. The app reads it entirely on your device — nothing is sent to any server.
  3. 3
    Set UTM Zone & Hemisphere
    This is the most important step for precision. For Cameroon, select zone 32N (Douala, Bafoussam, western regions) or 33N (Yaoundé, Bertoua, central/east regions). You can find the zone written on your survey plan or in AutoCAD drawing properties.
  4. 4
    Click Convert
    The app converts all your survey lines, polylines, and points into precise geographic coordinates using the WGS84 ellipsoid — the same standard as GPS.
  5. 5
    Download KML or KMZ
    Download KML to open directly in Google Earth desktop, or KMZ (compressed) to share by email or WhatsApp with colleagues. Both open with a double-click in Google Earth.
